Retrieve IP address in your network!

May 28, 2022

Use command below to search for SSH servers on your local network.

sudo nmap -Pn -p 22 -n 192.168.3.0/23 | less -p open

Then use / within less to search for the MAC address of the board. The IP address to use is the one printed above the MAC address. Then use q to quit less.
